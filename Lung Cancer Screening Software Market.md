**Diagnostic Modalities: Precision in Image-Based Intelligence**

**Low-Dose Computed Tomography (LDCT)**

LDCT remains the gold standard in lung cancer screening due to its ability to deliver high-resolution images with reduced radiation exposure. This modality is central to most national lung screening programs and benefits significantly from AI-enhanced interpretation that can detect even minute nodules at asymptomatic stages.

**Chest X-Rays**

While less sensitive than LDCT, chest X-rays are still widely used, especially in regions with limited access to advanced imaging equipment. The integration of AI overlays on chest X-rays is elevating their diagnostic value by highlighting suspicious lesions that may have been overlooked during manual interpretation.
