Romania is increasingly adopting green building standards such as LEED and BREEAM certifications. As environmental awareness grows and regulatory standards become stricter, there is a heightened demand for low-VOC and water-based adhesive products. Additionally, manufacturers are investing in bio-based and recyclable adhesive formulations to align with EU climate and sustainability goals.
