**Sustainable Aviation Fuel Market Growth Drivers**

Several key factors are propelling the rapid development of the SAF market:

- **Environmental Regulations**: Stringent policies like the EU's ReFuelEU Aviation Initiative, mandating a 2% SAF blend by 2025 and escalating to 70% by 2050, are compelling airlines to adopt sustainable fuels .
- **Technological Advancements**: Innovations in feedstock processing and refining technologies, including Ethanol-to-Jet (EtJ) and Fischer-Tropsch synthesis, are enhancing SAF production efficiency . 
- **Corporate Commitments**: Major airlines and aircraft manufacturers are investing heavily in SAF development to meet sustainability goals and consumer demand for greener travel options.

**Challenges**

Despite promising prospects, the SAF market faces several obstacles:

- **High Production Costs**: SAF remains significantly more expensive than traditional jet fuel, hindering widespread adoption. 
- **Feedstock Availability**: Ensuring a consistent and sustainable supply of feedstocks is critical for scaling SAF production.
- **Regulatory Hurdles**: Navigating complex international regulations and securing certifications for new SAF pathways pose challenges.
