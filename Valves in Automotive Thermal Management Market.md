**Valves in Automotive Thermal Management Market Segmentation**

**By Valve Type**

- **Thermostat Valves**: Regulate engine temperature by controlling coolant flow.
- **Coolant Control Valves**: Manage coolant distribution to various vehicle components.
- **Heater Control Valves**: Control the flow of coolant to the heater core, affecting cabin temperature.
- **EGR Valves**: Reduce NOx emissions by recirculating a portion of exhaust gases back into the engine cylinders.
- **Bypass Valves**: Allow coolant to bypass certain components under specific conditions.
- **Pressure Relief Valves**: Prevent excessive pressure buildup within the cooling system.
