` `**Deep Dive: Products & Services Landscape**

**1. Instruments**

Advanced instrumentation has become the cornerstone of modern anatomic pathology laboratories. Key products include:

- **Microtomes & Cryostats**: Crucial for precision tissue sectioning.
- **Tissue Processors**: Automate embedding of specimens in paraffin.
- **Automatic Stainers**: Enhance visibility of cellular structures through consistent staining.
- **Whole Slide Imaging (WSI) Scanners**: Digitize pathology slides for high-resolution analysis and telepathology.
- **Centrifuges, Microscopes, Embedding Centers**: Critical for supporting end-to-end diagnostic workflows.

**2. Consumables**

Recurrent materials vital for every pathology procedure:

- **Reagents & Antibodies**: Enable immunohistochemistry (IHC) and special stains for biomarker identification.
- **Probes & Kits**: Support molecular diagnostics including FISH and PCR-based assays.
- **Slides, Cover Slips, and Fixatives**: Ensure integrity and longevity of samples.

**3. Services**

Pathology services are essential for both clinical and research purposes:

- **Anatomic Pathology Services**: Focused on diagnostics through tissue/cell examination.
- **Consultation Services**: Expert second opinions and specialized interpretations.
- **Diagnostic Kits and Reagents**: Preconfigured tools to streamline and standardize testing protocols.
