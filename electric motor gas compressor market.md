**Segmentation Analysis**

**By Type of Compressor**

- **Centrifugal Compressors** (38.7% market share in 2023)
  - Ideal for high-volume continuous applications.
  - Widely used in **petrochemical plants, refineries, and power stations**.
- **Reciprocating Compressors**
  - Suitable for high-pressure, low-flow applications.
  - Common in **chemical manufacturing, gas transmission, and pharmaceutical sectors**.
- **Screw Compressors**
  - Known for steady airflow and quiet operation.
  - Suitable for applications in **HVAC systems, general manufacturing, and food & beverage industries**.

**By Power Rating**

- **Low Power (Up to 100 kW)**
  - Targeted for small workshops and specialized applications requiring compact and energy-efficient equipment.
- **Medium Power (100 kW – 1 MW)**
  - The largest segment by revenue in 2023.

- **High Power (Above 1 MW)**
  - Expected to grow at **CAGR of 5.6%**.

- **Chemical Industry**
  - Utilized for compressing reactive or inert gases used in **polymerization, synthesis, and reaction processes**.
- **Power Generation**
  - Helps maintain consistent pressure in **gas turbines and backup power systems**.
